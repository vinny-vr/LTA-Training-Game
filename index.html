LTA - Training Bootcamp

A lot of people were having questions about statlines for the upcoming Arima Kinen, so I decided to make this little game as a silly and fun way to help people figure out!

This follows the current (slightly simplified) version of the training rules, and will quickly take your uma through 21 months of her career. The career length assumes she began at the start of the school year (Mid Spring) during Year 1, moved to classics during Year 2, and will be running the Arima Kinen in Early Winter of Year 2. But you are free to customize how long or how short you want it.

Your uma will begin with 750 total stats, and every month will be worth a random amount of stat points. You can tweak the minimum and maximum possible training values for each month before starting, but recommended values were already entered for you. You're welcome! :D
